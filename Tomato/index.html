THE RESTAURANT

A little about us and a breif history of how we started.

Welcome to TOMATO, where Indian flavors meet vegetarian excellence. Our space blends tradition with modern elegance for a memorable dining experience. Explore a menu crafted by passionate chefs celebrating the richness of fresh, healthy vegetables. Whether for a quiet meal or joyous celebration, our warm ambiance awaits.

Indulge in the art of vegetarian dining at TOMATO, where good food and vibrant flavors converge in a welcoming atmosphere. Our menu, a fusion of classics and innovative creations, caters to every palate. Join us for an authentic, flavorful, and wholesome culinary journey.

TODAY'S SPECIAL

Today's Specials - A Symphony of Fresh Flavors and Daily Inspirations!

1. Desserts

Satisfy your sweet cravings with our irresistible desserts at Tomato. From classics to innovative treats, our dessert menu promises a delightful finish to your meal.

Indulge in the velvety richness of our signature Chocolate Mousse or try the fusion delight of Gulab Jamun Cheesecake. Our desserts are a perfect way to sweeten your dining experience, with each bite promising a symphony of flavors that will linger on your palate.

2. Main Course

Explore the rich flavors of India with our Main Course selection. Each dish is a journey through the diverse regions, bringing authentic tastes to your table.

Savor North Indian classics like Paneer Butter Masala or enjoy the crispiness of a Dosa Platter from the South. Our Thali experience offers a wholesome taste of Indian cuisine, ensuring that every dish is crafted with authentic spices and love.

3. Beverages

Quench your thirst with our refreshing beverages. From traditional favorites to modern mocktails, our drink menu complements every palate.

Try the invigorating Minty Mango Lassi or savor the bold flavor of Cold Brewed Coffee. Our handcrafted mocktails, like Tropical Breeze, add a vibrant touch to your dining experience, providing a refreshing accompaniment to your meal.

4. International Dishes

Embark on a global culinary adventure with our International Types menu. Enjoy the essence of Italian, Chinese, and more on your plate.

From classic Margherita Pizza to Vegetable Stir-Fry and creamy Fettuccine Alfredo, our international dishes invite you to explore diverse flavors from around the world. Each dish is a passport to a culinary adventure, delivering a global twist to your dining table.
